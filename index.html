General Rules

  • The Competition is open for the students of classes IX to XII.
  • Each school has to send one team comprising of three students.
  • Round 1 Elimination round. Online test of 20 question.
  • Each team are requested to bring their own smartphone and install The ‘Kahoot’ app which can be found on the Google Play Store and App Store
  • Top 6 team will be selected for the final round.
  • Quiz-master's decision will be final and binding to all.
  • Participants to be seated according to the Draw of lot.
  • Round one begin clockwise and round 2 goes anticlockwise to gave a fair chance to all the teams.


Topics for quiz will include:
  • Current Affairs
  • General Knowledge
  • Science
  • Sports
  • History & Geography
  • Technology
  • Space
  • Enviroment
  • Logical & Reasoning Abilities
  • Cinema & Entertainment
